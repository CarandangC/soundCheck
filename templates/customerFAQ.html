Hi there, welcome to the customer FAQ! How can we help?

To purchase tickets on our website, search for the event you're interested in and select the number of tickets you'd like to purchase. Then, proceed to checkout, where you'll be prompted to enter your payment and personal information.


Once you've purchased your ticket, you can expect to receive an email confirmation containing your ticket and information about the concert. This email will be sent to the email address that you provided when creating your account on our website. You can access your ticket by opening the email and downloading the attachment, which should be your ticket.


Some events may have additional fees or taxes added to the ticket price. You can review the total cost during the checkout process before completing your purchase.


In the event that an event is cancelled or rescheduled, we will do our best to notify all ticket holders via email and/or phone. Depending on the specific circumstances, we may be able to offer refunds or provide tickets to a rescheduled event.
